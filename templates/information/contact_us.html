FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days within Phnom Penh and 5–7 days nationwide. Express shipping is available at checkout for next-day delivery in the capital.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unworn items in original packaging. Simply contact us via email or phone and we'll arrange a pickup at no cost to you.
Are all products authentic?
100% yes. Every product sold on VRUN is sourced directly from authorized distributors. We provide certificates of authenticity for all premium brands.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also view real-time order status from your account's "My Orders" page.
Do you offer size exchanges?
Yes! If the size doesn't fit, we'll exchange it free of charge within 14 days. Contact our team and we'll make it right.
